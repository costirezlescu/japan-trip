Japan Travel Handbook

Tokyo Nikko Kanazawa & Takayama Kyoto Nara, Osaka & Hiroshima

Tokyo (June 6–9, 29–30)

Day 1 — June 6: Arrival

Visit Asakusa, Senso-ji Temple, and Nakamise Shopping Street.

Day 2 — June 7: Tokyo

Meiji Shrine, Yoyogi Park walk, Yanaka Ginza, and Ueno Park.

“Yanaka conserve une atmosphère d’avant-guerre, rare à Tokyo. Flânez dans ses ruelles bordées de maisons basses, de temples paisibles et de vieux commerces.” – Routard
Day 3 — June 8: Tokyo

Tea ceremony at Maikoya Tokyo, Edo-Tokyo Museum, Odaiba.

Day 4 — June 9: Nikko Day Trip

Toshogu Shrine, Kanmangafuchi Abyss, nature walk.

Day 24 — June 29: Tokyo Return

Kagurazaka, stay at Hotel Ryumeikan Ochanomizu Honten (Recommended by Cosmin).

Day 25 — June 30: Tokyo

Shibamata, Ginza, LaQua Onsen and final stroll.
